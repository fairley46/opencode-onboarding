# Module 12: Troubleshooting and Next Steps

## Why This Matters

Beginners should leave with a recovery path. If something goes wrong later, they should know what to check instead of assuming they are "bad at AI."

## The Simple Picture

Most problems come from one of a few things:

- the goal was unclear
- the context was incomplete
- the wrong tool was connected
- permissions were missing
- the question really needed a human answer

## A Practical Example

If the answer feels vague, do not just try again with the same prompt. Instead ask:

- did I give the right source?
- did I say what output I wanted?
- am I asking for something internal that needs a human answer?

That kind of reset is more productive than repeatedly guessing.

## Safety Check

When the task touches business policy, approvals, security, or sensitive systems, escalation is a strength, not a failure.

## What To Remember

Better prompting helps, but safe use also means knowing when to pause, narrow scope, or ask a person.
